## 1. What Data We Collect
- **Necessary Information:** Email address via Google OAuth, extracted payment metadata (payee name, amount, due date, status, Gmail message ID)
- **What We Never Collect/Store:** Email body/content, attachments, full recipient details, unrelated personal data

## 2. How Data is Used
- To provide dashboards of upcoming payments
- To improve payment tracking features and accuracy

## 3. Security Controls
- OAuth tokens stored only in encrypted Redis sessions
- No email is persisted; all processing is in-memory within the Context Engine
- TLS encryption used for all API/database communications

## 4. Your Rights
- View, export, or delete your payment data at any time
- Revoke Gmail access or delete your account whenever you wish

## 5. Third Parties
- Only uses Google APIs with minimum access required
- No data shared with external parties without explicit user consent

## 6. Cookies & Analytics
- May use cookies for session management and dashboard performance

## 7. Changes to Policy
- Any updates will be communicated within the app/dashboard
